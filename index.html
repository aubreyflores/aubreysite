How it works

Three steps to your AML risk report.

1. Connect
Link your TRON wallet through a secure interface. We only read public data.
2. Analyze
We analyze transfers, counterparties and protocol interactions, and cross‑check with public sanctions and risk lists.
3. Report
Receive a summary with overall risk and key factors in a user‑friendly format.

FAQ

Do I need to sign a transaction?
Connection may require a safe confirmation. We do not move funds or charge fees without approval.
What data is analyzed?
Public transactions, interactions with addresses/contracts, aggregated risk sources and open sanctions lists.
Is this financial advice?
No. The report is informational and helps evaluate general risk.
Can I check multiple addresses?
Yes. After connecting, choose the address you want to analyze.
